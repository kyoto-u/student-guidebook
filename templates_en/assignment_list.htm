How to Read the Assignemnt List

Click "Assignment" or "課題" from the left menu in the course site to show the list of assignments.

Description for Each Item

Click each item to sort the list.

  1. The title of the assignment/li>
  2. Status（such as "Not Started", "Draft - In progress", "Returned", "Submitted"）
  3. The open date of the assignment
  4. The due date of the assignment
The status will be "Not Started" if it has not been submitted, "Submitted" if it has been submitted.
Returned assignments are displayed as "Returned". Expired assignments are also displayed as "Not Started", however you cannot submit them.
The ongoing assignments (not submitted but opened) are displayed as "Draft - In progress".
