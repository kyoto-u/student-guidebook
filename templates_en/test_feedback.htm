How do I check test and quiz feedback?

To see your test and quiz grades and grader comments, please follow the steps bellow.
1. Click "Tests & Quizzes" in the left menu bar of each course site to move to "Tests & Quizzes" page.
2. Click "Feedback" on the test or quiz.
If there is no "Feedback" link：Feedback is not availabledue to the test and quiz settings.
3. View feedback.

The display changes depending on the test settings.
Correctness, score, and grader's comment are displayed.
