PandA Student Guide

What is PandA?

PandA is a learning management system launched by Kyoto University's Institute for Information Management and Communication in 2013.
In conjunction with classes, instructors and students can exchange assignments and class resources via the web.
In addition, students can access Zoom meeting for remote classes from PandA.
A student account (ECS-ID) is required to use PandA.
There are some precautions when using PandA. Please read here carefully.
